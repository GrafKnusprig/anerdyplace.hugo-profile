About Me

When you ask someone to tell you about themselves, it’s never easy.

I’ve been interested in all sorts of things all my life and my interests are constantly changing. I live to evolve and discover new joys in life. If I had to describe myself in one word, it would be that I am an artist.

After school, I studied computer science and now have over 10 years of professional experience as a full stack software engineer.

Since one skill alone does not satisfy me, I have many hobbies. I enjoy personalizing everything in my life, adapting things and processes to my needs. Here I can live out my creativity to the full.

My passions also include art and music. I often do small favors for my friends. Whether it’s 3D models, logos, programs, websites, videos or photoshoots… the list is truly endless.

Whenever possible, I make my designs and programs open source. I’m a big advocate of that.


On this website I want to give an overview of my projects. Unfortunately, I can’t keep up with posting everything I do here. But I’ll try to post something from time to time.

FAQ: Why the nickname raven? - Well, I like ravens. ;)
