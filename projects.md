## Mini Retro Cam
- small and convenient, fast digital videocamera
```txt
Short version: for a 1/4” (≈3.2 × 2.4 mm, 4:3) sensor, a 35 mm full-frame equivalent is about a 3.2 mm lens.

That gives you roughly:
	•	HFOV ≈ 53°
	•	VFOV ≈ 41°
	•	DFOV ≈ 63°

So if the module lists angles, aim for ~53° horizontal (or ~63° diagonal). If it lists focal length, grab something around 3.2 mm.
```
